# Hackathon Social Playbook

> Generate a personalized X strategy for newly submitted Colosseum hackathon projects — built on real pattern analysis of grand prize winners across 4 hackathons.

## What This Skill Does

Most hackathon teams ship great products but fail at storytelling. Of 12 prominent Colosseum winners analyzed, only ~5 maintain a discoverable, active X presence. A project with even moderate X discipline gets disproportionate ecosystem visibility relative to product quality.

This skill generates a complete, copy-paste-ready 30-day social playbook based on what actually worked for ORE, Unruggable, Tapedrive, Supersize, and DeCharge.

## Input (What You Provide)

1. Project name
2. What your project does (1–2 sentences)
3. Who it's for (target audience)
4. Tech stack
5. Team background (optional)
6. Hackathon status: just submitted / waiting for results / we won / didn't win but shipping

## Output (What You Get)

1. **Handle & Bio** — Defensible handle format + bio under 160 characters using the winner formula
2. **Core Story Angle** — One of 5 proven patterns (Category Claiming, Credential Stacking, Problem→Solution Arc, First Mover, Resilience Arc) with primary + backup angle
3. **4-Week Content Calendar** — Week-by-week plan with follower targets: Week 1 (50–100), Week 2 (100–250), Week 3 (10–20 engagement/post), Week 4 (500+ profile visits)
4. **Daily Engagement Checklist** — Morning / midday / evening with time estimates (15–10–15 min)
5. **Win Announcement Playbook** — Template, same-hour actions, 72-hour follow-up schedule, visual asset guide (Canva, 1200×675px)
6. **First 5 Posts** — Written out in full, copy-paste ready, calibrated to your project
7. **Quick-Start Checklist** — "Do these TODAY" to get moving in under an hour

## Research Foundation

Pattern analysis across 4 Colosseum hackathons — Renaissance '24, Radar '24, Cypherpunk '25, Breakout '25:

| Project | Result | Followers | Posts | Key pattern |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| ORE | Grand Prize, Renaissance '24 | 43,900 | 2,347 | Category claiming — "Solana's store of value token" |
| Unruggable | Grand Prize, Cypherpunk '25 | 6,559 | 258 | Win announcement = 37K impressions, 350 likes |
| Tapedrive | Grand Prize, Breakout '25 | 2,259 | 182 | 6 posts/week average, registered same month as hackathon |
| Supersize | 1st Gaming, Radar '24 | 1,426 | 231 | Consistent build-in-public cadence |

## Key Insights Baked In

- Register your handle the day the hackathon opens — not the day of submission
- Tagging order matters: partner protocol → @solana → @Colosseum → @superteam (partner first triggers their RT)
- Win announcements are your single highest-leverage post — treat them like a product launch
- Consistent posting (3–5x/week) during the build beats bursty posting after the win
- Custom graphics at 1200×675px consistently outperform text-only win posts
- Reply to small accounts (<1K followers) as much as big ones — they remember it

## Use Cases

- Pre-submission: build your narrative foundation while building product
- Post-submission: execute social strategy while waiting for results
- Post-win: leverage the credential into lasting ecosystem presence
- Didn't win: ship anyway — the "we're shipping regardless" post earns real goodwill
