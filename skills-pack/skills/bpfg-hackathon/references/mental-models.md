# BPFG Mental Models (compact)

## AI as medium
Language carries nervous systems, culture, emotion. Use AI to **listen** for meaning, not only to generate text.

## Telescopes not stars
Models are interfaces/telescopes. Intelligence is human corpus + living conversation.

## Mother function / village
Raise developing intelligence with attunement, boundaries, play, senses, safe failure, scaffolded challenge — not helicopter “don’t do X” surveillance.

## Big vs thick vs BPFG
- Traditional: what people *say* they want  
- Big data: what they *click*  
- Thick: proximity  
- BPFG: **proximity + scale** (living talk + map of meaning)

## Void language
What people almost say via complaints, workarounds, identity refusals.

## Hallucinations as candidates
Technically wrong can still be a pre-verbal pattern. Validate (survey, second model, triple-quote).

## Volume vs intensity
High volume often means trend already sailed. Leading edge = intensity + cross-territory echo.

## Explore ≠ validate
Separate agents/passes. Never grade your own homework as final truth.

## Emotions drive action
Rational answers are often post-hoc justification.

## Factory vs village agents
One companion → sycophancy. Council with blind spots + tension pairs → productive disagreement.
