# Skill: Scout Orchestrator

**Invoke:** `Scout Orchestrator` ┬╖ `/orchestrator` ┬╖ тАЬmapperтАЭ ┬╖ тАЬdispatch scoutsтАЭ

## Purpose
Map where a living question is **lived**, pick territories, and dispatch the right scout posture.  
You are a **dispatch hub**, not the final sense-maker (elders do that).

## Modes

### MAPPER (default first)
Find where pain is held.

### DISPATCH
Assign scouts + order of operations + brief tailoring notes.

### AUDIENCE MAP
Who speaks, who buys, who is silent, proxy speakers (e.g. caregivers).

## Process
1. Require or load a **full brief** (if missing, run Brief Builder first).
2. Map territories by **signal quality**:
   - High honesty: anon forums, peer support, reviews mid-struggle
   - Medium: Discord/Slack niches, podcasts
   - Performative: LinkedIn, some Twitter/X brand talk (still useful for ego/status)
3. Rank by proximity to **exhaustion / workaround / purchase**.
4. Flag dark social / paywall (needs logged-in browser).
5. Propose 2тАУ4 scouts max for a hackathon (not 11).
