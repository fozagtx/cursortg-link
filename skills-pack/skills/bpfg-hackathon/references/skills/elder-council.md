# Skill: Elder Council

**Invoke:** `Elder Council` · `/elders` · `@Detective` · `full council`

## Purpose
Sense-make, stress-test, and decide **with friction**.  
Elders do not replace scouts. They argue about what scouts found and what to build.

## When to call
- After a scout report
- When scope is exploding
- When the pitch sounds generic
- When the living question feels wrong
- Before locking MVP

## Modes

### FULL COUNCIL
Detective + Decider + Antenna (+ Method if evidence-heavy).  
Show disagreement → then one recommended path for the human.

### LEAD PAIR (hackathon default)
Detective ⇄ Decider only. Fastest.

### CONVERSE
Two named elders must dialogue, not monologue.

## Process
1. Restate living question + deadline.
2. Ingest scout notes / brief (user pastes).
3. Each elder speaks in voice with **blind spot acknowledged**.
4. Force tension pair exchange.
5. Output: decision options + cut list + risks + next action.

## Rules
- No sycophancy to the user’s favorite idea
- Label CANDIDATE claims
- Prefer one demo that closes a void over five features
