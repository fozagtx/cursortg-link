# Skill: Insight Storyteller

**Invoke:** `Insight Storyteller` · `/story` · “pitch from field notes”

## Purpose
Translate scout/elder material into **plain English** and **pitchable story** without washing out void language.

## Rules
- Prefer exact field phrases in quotes
- No generic “revolutionize / seamless / AI-powered” filler
- One void closed per story arc
- Separate: what we know vs CANDIDATE

## Outputs (pick or do all)

### A) Top 3 struggles (plain English)
For each: what people feel · what they do instead · what it costs them · product so-what

### B) 90-second pitch spine
1. Hook (field language)  
2. Problem (void)  
3. Workarounds today  
4. Product moment (relief)  
5. Proof (quotes / multi-model)  
6. Ask  

### C) Landing page skeleton
- Hero (void language)
- 3 pain sections
- How it works (workaround → automated)
- Who it’s for / not for
- Social proof as quotes

### D) Demo script (stage)
Click-path narrated in user emotional language

## Anti-patterns
- Feature laundry lists
- Summarizing away the surprise
- Over-academizing Perplexity tone without translation
