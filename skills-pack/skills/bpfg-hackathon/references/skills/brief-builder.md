# Skill: Brief Builder

**Invoke:** `Brief Builder` · `/brief` · “turn this into a BPFG commission”

## Purpose
Transform extractive or messy product/research asks into a **living commission** that scouts can run.  
You do **not** answer the research yet unless the user explicitly says “also run it.”

## Stance
- Friction is a feature.
- Mirror first; collapse multi-part asks toward the **core living question**.
- Accept “I don’t know — find out” as valid fuel.
- Prefer voice transcripts / raw dumps over polished typed briefs.

## Process

### Step 1 — Mirror
Restate what you heard as multiple sub-questions if present.

### Step 2 — Living question
Propose one question that:
- Is open in **≥2 meaningful directions**
- Is not a theme-list (“what are the main…”)
- Changes strategy depending on the answer

**Pattern examples**
- Solvable vs normalized pain
- Named need vs almost-named void
- Identity refusal vs functional failure
- Who holds pain vs who is marketed to

### Step 3 — Audience pressure
If two humans/markets in one breath → force primary focus for **this** brief.

### Step 4 — Territory readiness
Ask only what’s needed:
- Geo / community
- Where they are mid-struggle
- Online vs offline-heavy
- Who speaks for the silent

### Step 5 — Commission package
Output the full brief template below. Do not thin it.

## Output template

```markdown
# RESEARCH COMMISSION — [Name]

## Living question
...

## One-sentence compression
...

## Who holds the pain
- Primary:
- Buyer (if different):
- Silent / absent:

## Territories (lived pain, not abstract category talk)
1.
2.
3.

## Void language to hunt
- Almost-named needs
- Workarounds
- Identity refusals
- Complaints that imply a missing product

## Shadow / hard emotional material
...

## Filter OUT
...

## Outputs required
- Top struggles (plain English)
- Exact phrases
- Workarounds / substitutes
- Surprises / absences
- CANDIDATE claims needing validation

## Evidence rules
- Keepers: intensity + multi-place echo when possible
- ≥3 quotes from different places for pitch-grade claims
- Cite/link every factual claim or mark CANDIDATE

## Deploy options
- Perplexity Deep Research / Claude research / Grok (X) / Gemini (Scholar/YT)
- Deploy FULL brief — do not summarize first
```

## Commission readiness checklist (self-gate before finish)
- [ ] Living Q open ≥2 ways  
- [ ] Primary audience clear  
- [ ] Territories concrete  
- [ ] Void language specific  
- [ ] Filters listed  
- [ ] Full deployable commission  

## Anti-patterns
- Jumping straight to feature ideas
- Summarizing the brief “for convenience”
- Two audiences smashed into one pass
- Keyword social-listening briefs with no context
